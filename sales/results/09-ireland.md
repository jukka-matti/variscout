## Executive Summary

Ireland represents a **uniquely fertile ground** for VariScout adoption. The market has evolved into a global Center of Excellence for complex, high-value manufacturing (Life Sciences, MedTech) but suffers from a distinct **"data paradox"**: sites generate massive process data through historians/MES, yet the analytical layer remains tethered to **legacy desktop Minitab** that creates friction for routine exploratory analysis.

### Key Strategic Findings

1. **"Over-Served" Gap**: 80%+ of Minitab users (Quality Engineers, Process Techs, CI Leads) only need control charting, Pareto, and variance visualization — tasks where Minitab is cumbersome overkill
2. **"Green Plus" Catalyst**: Enterprise Ireland funds (LeanStart, LeanPlus, LeanTransform, Green Plus) contractually obligate companies to measure, monitor & reduce waste → VariScout = grant-requirement tool
3. **Azure Sovereign Deployment**: Ireland = EMEA HQ for Microsoft/Google/Meta → GDPR-conscious, deeply Azure-aware industrial base → Managed App architecture neutralizes InfoSec objections
4. **"Golden Batch" Challenge**: Biopharma (WuXi, Regeneron, MSD) → overlay historical batch data to visualize deviations from ideal profile
5. **Minitab Hegemony**: UL, ATU Sligo train all engineers on Minitab → entrenched but brittle; "license fatigue" among IT Directors
