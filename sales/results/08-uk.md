## Executive Summary

The UK market presents a **"Goldilocks" opportunity** for VariScout. The market is mature enough to understand the value of variation analysis (decades of Six Sigma from Toyota, Nissan, Rolls-Royce) but currently **over-paying for legacy tools** or **under-equipped with spreadsheets**.

### Key Strategic Findings

1. **"Minitab Audit" Strategy**: 80/20 rule — 80% of Minitab users (Green Belts, Process Engineers) only use basic charting; only 20% (MBBs) use advanced features (DOE, Reliability). Replacing 15 of 20 licenses saves £20K+/yr.
2. **Azure "Trojan Horse"**: Tata Steel, INEOS, Unilever, AstraZeneca have public Azure case studies → path of least resistance.
3. **Data Sovereignty**: Aerospace/Defence (Rolls-Royce, BAE) → Managed App architecture = board-level trust builder.
4. **Margin Squeeze**: F&B sector (Bakkavor, Greencore) → "giveaway" reduction = immediate ROI.
