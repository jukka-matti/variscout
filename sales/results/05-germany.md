## Executive Summary

Germany's Mittelstand navigates a "Quality-Cost Paradox": maintaining "Made in Germany" quality while under historic cost pressure (energy prices, Fachkräftemangel, Asian competition). Minitab is entrenched but its licensing model and desktop architecture create friction for modernizing CI teams. Industry 4.0 transition prioritizes cloud connectivity, democratized data access, and browser-based collaboration.
