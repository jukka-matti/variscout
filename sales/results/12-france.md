## Executive Summary

The French industrial landscape is navigating a profound transformation driven by **"France 2030"** re-industrialization and rapid IT modernization. A distinct bifurcation exists: **Global Enterprises (GEs)** aggressively migrating to Azure (Sanofi, Saint-Gobain), and **ETIs** (mid-market) under OEM pressure to digitize quality reporting but lacking capital for monolithic desktop software.

### Key Strategic Findings

1. **"Citizen Data Scientist" Mandate**: Skills gap in advanced statistics → shop-floor empowerment over "Black Belt priesthood" → browser-based accessibility is the answer
2. **"Industrie du Futur" / Usine 4.0**: GIFAS pushing digital continuity → CSV→Minitab→PDF workflow breaks the digital thread → VariScout as connector
3. **Azure France Central**: Data sovereignty via Azure France regions → neutralizes "cloud souverain" concerns
4. **"Yellow Belt" Mass Market**: Thousands of process engineers need Control Charts & Pareto — not DOE → VariScout as the "Yellow Belt Tool"
5. **ETI Supply Chain Entry**: Small ETIs (Figeac Aéro, Akwel) need to report quality to GEs but can't afford Minitab for every engineer
