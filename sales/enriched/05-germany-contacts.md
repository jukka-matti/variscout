## German-Specific Outreach Notes

1. **Use German in initial outreach** — KVP Manager ≠ CI Manager in cultural weight
2. **Reference certifications** — IATF 16949, ISO 13485, VDA 6.3 resonate more than "Lean"
3. **XING still relevant** — Especially for traditional Mittelstand engineers; check both platforms
4. **Data sovereignty** — Always mention Azure Germany West Central (Frankfurt) region
5. **Betriebsrat** — Be aware: Works Council may need to approve new software tools
