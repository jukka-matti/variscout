Naming brainstorm (2026-06-09) concluded: **Luuppi** (Finnish for _loupe_, the precision lens) proposed as the rebrand of **VariScout**. **NOT adopted — the product name remains VariScout** until two gates clear: (1) a say-it-aloud user test (the "loopy / loo-pee" risk), (2) formal EU/Finland trademark clearance. Adoption + status live in `docs/decision-log.md` (2026-06-09 entry) + the concept doc `docs/superpowers/specs/2026-06-09-luuppi-naming-concept-design.md` — **NOT here**.

**Why Luuppi:** lands on the product's true center ("see into the process" — a loupe IS that instrument) + carries "treasure in the spread" (you loupe a gem to find the value in the detail others average away) + the _ē-vidēre_ etymology (evidence = "made visible") unifies see-and-prove; authentically Finnish yet it _travels_ (the _loupe_ bridge — unlike naakka/näätä). Manifesto = Tukey's _"notice what you never expected to see."_ "Evidence" kept as internal vocabulary (Evidence Map, _evidenced_ — already in code); CoScout→**Evi** and .vrs→.evd optional later. Brands the product identity in [[project_wedge_v1]].

**Runners-up reserved (if Luuppi fails its gates):** Tracer (clear+distinct+global bridge), Jackdaw (clean characterful animal), Salient (Tukey-literal). Rule: don't trade down to another generic — stay VariScout deliberately if Luuppi fails.

**Rejected + why (don't relitigate):** Exploria ($750M Exploria Resorts owns TM/domain/SEO); Evidento (real-user feedback "sounds dental" — buried evi-**DENT**-o + the -o reads cartoon); Lode (telecom co + "load" homophone); Trove/Assay/Magpie/Heron/Kestrel/Dipper/Rook/Goshawk (all taken in analytics SaaS); Murmur/Murmuration (Bloomberg civic-data nonprofit + 5 startups; also emergence ≠ causal investigation); Sterna/Susurra (off-center emergence). Domains: grab luuppi.io/.ai (luuppi.fi = Tampere student org).

**Method lessons (durable):** (1) **Availability is the decisive filter** — most "obvious" good names are already taken in analytics; ownable territory = the less-obvious word (the only clean keen-eyed animal was Jackdaw). (2) **Real-user feedback beats armchair reasoning** (IDEO "test on humans") — one tester's "dental" killed Evidento after all our reasoning missed it; the say-aloud test is the veto gate. (3) **Grounding the name in what the product actually IS** (re-read OVERVIEW/glossary mid-session) course-corrected away from pretty-but-wrong metaphors (murmuration = emergence, not the causal-investigation bullseye).
