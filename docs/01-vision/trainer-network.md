# Trainer Network

VariScout's ecosystem model is a **lightweight trainer network**, not a franchise and not a heavyweight reseller program.

The purpose is simple:

- spread the VariScout methodology without founder travel
- make it easy for customers to find trusted trainers and consultants
- keep certification authority close enough to prevent quality drift
- let the network grow through people, not through territorial deals

---

## Core Model

There are only two certified statuses:

### Trainer

A certified individual allowed to deliver VariScout training and consulting to customers.

### Master Trainer

A senior certified individual allowed to certify new Trainers using the official VariScout trainer-certification process.

Certification belongs to **people**, not companies.

Companies may appear in the directory only as a **grouping and contact route** when one or more active Trainers work there.

---

## What This Is Not

VariScout does **not** start with:

- country leads
- exclusive territories
- franchise fees
- formal partner tiers
- annual recertification exams for all Trainers

These may be revisited later if the network becomes large enough to justify them, but they are not part of the initial operating model.

---

## Operating Rules

### Trainer Certification

- New Trainers are certified by a Master Trainer using the official VariScout materials and assessment rubric.
- Trainers may deliver official VariScout training and customer-facing consulting after certification.
- Trainer status remains active without annual re-examination.

### Trainer Maintenance

Trainers complete a light yearly attestation confirming that:

- they are still active
- their profile details are current
- they accept the current program terms
- they use current official VariScout materials

Trainer status may be suspended or removed if the trainer becomes inactive for an extended period, misuses the brand, repeatedly delivers poor-quality training, or refuses mandatory update training after a major methodology or product change.

### Master Trainer Renewal

Master Trainers renew annually.

To keep Master Trainer status, they must:

- run at least one trainer-certification course every 12 months
- remain in good standing on delivery quality
- accept updated standards and materials

Becoming a Master Trainer always requires explicit VariScout approval. It is not automatic based on tenure.

---

## Materials Policy

VariScout provides official:

- slide decks
- exercises
- trainer notes
- participant handouts
- certification rubrics

Trainers may customize delivery materials for:

- customer industry
- local language
- local examples
- workshop context

They may not change the core VariScout methodology, certification criteria, or official product claims.

---

## Directory Model

All active Trainers and Master Trainers are listed on the VariScout website.

Each profile should show:

- name
- status: Trainer or Master Trainer
- country
- languages
- company, if applicable
- contact route
- active or inactive status

If multiple active Trainers belong to the same company, the directory groups them and may show a company-level contact route such as **Contact this company for training**.

If a company no longer has any active Trainers, it loses grouped visibility.

---

## Why This Model

This model is intentionally smaller than a classic channel program.

It fits VariScout better because:

- the product is methodology-led, so trust sits with the person delivering it
- a solo consultant can participate without needing firm-level accreditation
- quality control is simpler than with country franchises
- the ecosystem can grow internationally without founder travel becoming the bottleneck
- company grouping still creates a clean commercial signal for customers

The design principle is **central standards, distributed delivery**.

---

## Success Criteria

The trainer network is working if:

- customers can find local Trainers without direct founder involvement
- partner-led training drives adoption of the PWA and Azure plans
- Master Trainers create new Trainers without quality drift
- independent trainers and company-based trainers can both participate
- the website directory stays current with low operational overhead

If the network grows large enough that language coverage, localization, or market development needs formal structure, VariScout can add optional company accreditation or geography-specific roles later.
