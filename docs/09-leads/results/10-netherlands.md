## Executive Summary

The Netherlands, particularly the **Brainport Eindhoven** region, represents a premier target market due to its high concentration of **"High-Mix, Low-Volume" (HMLV)** high-tech manufacturing. This environment inherently struggles with process variation that standard SPC tools fail to diagnose during the exploratory phase. The Dutch industrial sector exhibits high Azure adoption and LSS maturity, creating seamless technical and cultural fit.

### Key Strategic Findings

1. **"80/20" Value Proposition**: 80% analytical power for 20% of cost → democratize beyond certified Black Belts
2. **HMLV Imperative**: Brainport firms (VDL, NTS, Prodrive) produce in batches of 10-100, not 100,000 → standard SPC charts ill-suited → exploratory analysis fits perfectly
3. **"Polder Model" Cultural Fit**: Dutch consensus-based decision-making → flat-fee unlimited users aligns with egalitarian culture of cross-functional problem-solving
4. **Azure Dominance**: FrieslandCampina, Heineken, Vanderlande standardized on Microsoft stack → Azure Managed App bypasses security audits
5. **"Smart Industry" Agenda**: Government-backed Industry 4.0 → digitization of factory floor in full swing
