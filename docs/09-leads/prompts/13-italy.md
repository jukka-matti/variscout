# VariScout Lead Research — Italy

Copy and paste the prompt below into Gemini Deep Research.

---

I'm researching potential customers for a process variation analysis tool called VariScout. The tool helps manufacturing and process industry teams understand WHERE variation comes from — so they know where to focus their continuous improvement efforts. It's NOT an SPC monitoring tool — it's an exploratory data analysis tool for process improvement.

Key facts about VariScout:

- Browser-based, no installation needed
- €150/month flat fee for unlimited users (vs Minitab at ~€155/user/month)
- Deployed via Azure Marketplace as a Managed Application
- Data stays in the customer's own Azure tenant
- Target users: CI/OpEx teams, quality engineers, Lean Six Sigma practitioners

The tool competes for the segment of users who currently use only 20% of Minitab's features (control charts, boxplots, capability analysis, Pareto) but pay 100% of the price. Larger teams = bigger cost savings.

**Research task for Italy:**

Italy is Europe's second-largest manufacturing country by output. Its industrial districts (distretti industriali) are dense clusters of specialized manufacturers. Please find at least 50 Italian companies that would be strong candidates for VariScout.

## CRITICAL OUTPUT FORMAT RULES:

1. **80% of your output must be the company table.** Minimal narrative — focus on finding companies.
2. **Use TABLE format** for all companies — one row per company, no paragraph descriptions.
3. **Score each company on ALL 5 criteria separately** (not a single combined score).
4. **Include LinkedIn company page URL** for each company.
5. **I need at LEAST 50 companies.** Include mid-sized manufacturers from industrial districts.

## Company criteria:

1. Manufacturing companies (discrete or process manufacturing), food production, pharmaceutical companies, or other process-intensive industries
2. Companies that likely have a Continuous Improvement, Operational Excellence, or Lean Six Sigma program
3. Companies with 50+ employees (larger is better — more potential Minitab licenses to replace)
4. Companies that are likely Microsoft/Azure users (bonus, not required)

## Output table format (one row per company):

| # | Company | Industry | Employees | CI/OpEx Evidence | Azure/Cloud | Best Contact Titles | LinkedIn URL | S1 | S2 | S3 | S4 | S5 | TOTAL |

Where scores S1-S5 are (each 1-5):

**Sort the entire table by TOTAL score descending.**
