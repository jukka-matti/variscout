## Austrian-Specific Outreach Notes

1. **karriere.at** is the primary job portal (equivalent to LinkedIn Jobs in AT)
2. **XING** still widely used — especially for traditional industry
3. **"Technik" culture** — respect engineering precision; VariScout = Ingenieurtool
4. **Data Sovereignty** — always mention: "Data stays in your Azure tenant"
5. **Betriebsrat** — Works Council may need to approve procurement (same as Germany)
6. **HTL graduates** — high baseline stats knowledge; they understand σ already
