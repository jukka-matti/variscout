## The Content Engine

```
1 VIDEO (Jukkis talking, 5-10 min)
        │
        ├── YouTube (full video)
        ├── Blog post (transcript + expansion)
        ├── LinkedIn posts (2-3 per week)
        ├── TikTok clips (3-5 per video)
        └── Instagram (reels + carousel)
```

One video creates 10+ pieces of content across platforms.
