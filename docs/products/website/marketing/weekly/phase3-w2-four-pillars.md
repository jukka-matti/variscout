## LinkedIn Posts

### Post 1: The Gemba Moment (Monday)

```
I was standing in a coffee processing facility in Africa.

The data I'd been staring at for hours suddenly made sense.

"See that pile of beans waiting? That's your 'holding time' column."
"See how this batch goes faster? That's why Tuesday's numbers look different."
"See that variation in the drying beds? That's your moisture spread."

The spreadsheet became real.
The data became physical.
The patterns had causes I could point at.

That's what the gemba teaches.

You can't truly understand process data without understanding the process.

#Gemba #ProcessImprovement #QualityManagement
```

```
What the African gemba taught me:

The data is not the process.

Take the tool to the floor.
```
